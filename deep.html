DEEP ROCK GALACTIC

Deep Rock Galactic is a cooperative first-person shooter video game. It's a 1-4 player co-op FPS featuring badass space Dwarves. The gameplay primarily centers around missions that take place in fully destructible, procedurally generated cave systems and endless hordes of alien monsters; these can have varying terrain generation, objectives, hazards, and enemies depending on the selected biome and mission type.
